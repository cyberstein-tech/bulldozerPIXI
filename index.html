About BulldozerPIXI

BulldozerPIXI v0.1 by George Moo at Cyberstein Technologies.

BulldozerPIXI is a half-baked recreation of the Bulldozer game series created by John 'FlyMan' Hattan (The Code Zone). The updated game series by the original author is available fully-featured for mobile platforms at https://www.thecodezone.com/game/bulldozer.html.
